Medical Robotics

Robots in this field are used in surgery, diagnosis and rehabilitation of patients. They can be teleconverts, as well as specialized medical instruments..

Industrial Robotics

These robots are used in production lines and factories to automate tasks such as assembly, welding, paint, etc. They usually have manipulators and can perform repetitive actions.

Robotics in the military sphere

Robots can be used for military purposes for reconnaissance, disposal of explosive devices and other tasks.

Robotics for scientific purposes

This field includes robots used in research of the underwater world, space, medicine and other fields of science.

Robots in Education

Robots are used to teach and develop the skills of children and students in schools and universities.

Robotics in the aerospace industry

Robots are used to assemble and maintain spacecraft and stations.
