RoboticsWorld

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field of engineering and science that involves the design, construction, programming, and use of robots. Robots are autonomous or semi-autonomous machines that can perform tasks with varying degrees of complexity, often with the goal of replacing or assisting humans in tasks that are dangerous, repetitive, or impractical for humans to perform.

Where it is used

Assistance to people with disabilities

Robot assistants can help people with physical or cognitive disabilities to perform daily tasks and make their lives easier.

Medicine

Medical robotics allows performing complex surgical operations with high accuracy and less invasiveness. Robots can also help in rehabilitation and patient care.

Environmental protection

Robots are used to clean the environment, monitor pollution and control agricultural pests.Robots-underwater vehicles (ROV) and autonomous underwater vehicles (AUV) are used for research and cleaning of marine and oceanic bioecosystems
